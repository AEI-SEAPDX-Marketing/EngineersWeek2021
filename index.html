A Big Geeky Thank-You from AEI Seattle / Portland

Dear Friends,

A year has gone by and another National Engineers' Week is upon us!! And what a year it has been!! As we have adapted to working remotely, new technologies and virtual teaming have become the norm. Through all of this, we at AEI have truly missed our in-person interactions with each of you. However, in resolving to make the best of things, we have found a silver lining in all of this. As you can see, working remotely has freed us to embrace our inner engineering tendencies to the fullest!! So this Engineers' Week we encourage you to put those cell phone holsters, plastic pocket protectors, and all the plaid you have to good use and celebrate (virtually) with us!!!

We truly value your friendship and look forward to seeing you in person soon.

Stay well. Stay safe.
